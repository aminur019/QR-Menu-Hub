How QRMenuHub Works

Simplifying your business operations with three easy steps.

Sign Up & Customize Your Menu

Create an account and easily upload or customize your menu to suit your business. Whether you’re a restaurant, bakery, or salon, QRMenuHub lets you design a digital menu tailored to your brand.

Generate QR Code

Generate a personalized QR code for your menu. This QR code can be placed on tables, counters, or your website, making it easy for customers to access your menu instantly with a simple scan.

Share & Engage Customers

Place your QR codes in your business—on tables, countertops, menus, or digital displays. Customers scan the code to access your up-to-date, interactive digital menu anytime, anywhere.
